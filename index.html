About me

I’m currently pursuing my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence Engineering – Biomedical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I serve as a BME Master’s Student Ambassador.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Biomedical Engineering through the joint UNC–Chapel Hill and NC State program. I fell in love with machine learning during a neuroscience research internship analyzing mouse behavior. That spark led me to CMU, where this program perfectly blends my background and ambition.

My long-term goal is to leave a meaningful mark on the world. Every time I have a machine learning project idea, I write it down. I want to be someone who uses AI to make an impact not just within industry, but society itself. I think the best applications of AI aren’t necessarily the most technologically advanced, but the most creative. In that sense, I think AI is definitely as much of an art as it is a science.
