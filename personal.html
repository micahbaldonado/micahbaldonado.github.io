Life Beyond Academics

I'm deeply interested in philosophy and psychology, exploring topics related to consciousness, mindfulness, and the nature of human experience. These interests help shape my worldview and influence how I approach my work in AI and biomedical engineering.

Outside of academics, I immerse myself in piano, drawing, poetry, and photography. Each of these activities allows me to explore creativity and expression in unique ways. I practice meditation regularly, finding balance and clarity in mindfulness. Additionally, I maintain an active lifestyle through regular workouts and enjoy the challenge and stimulation of learning new languages.

In my leisure time, I love engaging with strategic and competitive games such as Super Smash Bros and chess, appreciating the strategic depth and community interaction they offer.

Poetry

Poetry provides me a means to distill thoughts and emotions into concise, impactful expressions. It’s an art form through which I explore personal reflections, capture fleeting moments, and express ideas that resonate deeply with me.

Photography

Photography allows me to see the world through a different lens—literally and figuratively. Capturing images encourages me to slow down and appreciate the beauty and complexity of everyday scenes. I particularly enjoy experimenting with composition and lighting to tell visual stories that evoke emotion and contemplation.
